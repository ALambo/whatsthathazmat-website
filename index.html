HOW IT WORKS

Three steps.
Instant answers.

No training required. No manual lookup. Just point, scan, and know.

📷
01
PHOTOGRAPH THE PLACARD

Use your camera or upload an existing photo. The app handles any angle, lighting condition, or image quality. Multiple placards in one image are detected automatically.

🧠
02
AI IDENTIFIES THE HAZARD

Our AI reads the diamond shape, color, symbol, UN number, and hazard class — then cross-references the DOT database to identify the exact material and danger level.

🚨
03
GET ACTIONABLE INFORMATION

Receive the hazard class, UN number, danger level, common examples of the material, and clear emergency response guidance — all in plain English, not technical jargon.

⭐
PRO
GO DEEPER WITH PRO

Pro users also get historic accident data, the full DOT Emergency Response Guidebook, nearby hospital and HAZMAT team locations, regulatory compliance info, and incident reporting.
